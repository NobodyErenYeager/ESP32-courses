This course is centered around ESP32 board made by adafruit. This board is also called as Huzzah32 board by adafruit.

The reason for choosing this one is, its available across the world

AND

It works.

Believe me, I've experimented with almost every other esp32 board which is out there and each one comes with some issue of its own. Some boards have issue while downloading some have issue with pinouts.

Adafruit Huzzah32 Board just works without any issue and is perfectly suitable for industrial projects as well

You can buy adafruit Huzzah32 Board from Adafruit or a local distributor in your country

Apart from Huzzah32, we're going to use

  • Huzzah32 Feather Board (mentioned again)

  • DHT22 Sensor

  • Transistorized Relay Module

  • Breadboard

  • Connecting wires male to male and female to male

  • USB to Micro C Cable for downloading Programs

  • Few LEDs and switches
